Investing Basics

Quick primer on investing instruments and principles.

Stocks vs Bonds

Stocks: ownership in a company — higher potential returns, higher risk. Bonds: loans to issuers — lower risk, steady income.

Diversification

Spread investments across asset classes and regions to reduce risk.

Time horizon

Match investments to goals: short-term vs long-term. Use safer assets for near-term goals.

Costs

Watch fees (expense ratios, brokerage). Low-cost index funds are good for many investors.
