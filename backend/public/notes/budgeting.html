How to Build a Simple Budget

This short note explains a practical, easy budget you can start today.

1. Track your income

List your monthly net income (salary after tax, side incomes).

2. List fixed expenses

Rent, utilities, loan payments, subscriptions — amounts that don't change much each month.

3. Estimate variable expenses

Groceries, transport, entertainment — estimate average monthly spend.

4. Apply a simple rule

Use the 50/30/20 guideline as a starting point: 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings/debt repayment.

5. Make a plan and review weekly

Put automated transfers for savings if possible and review actuals weekly to adjust.

One actionable tip

Start with a small automatic transfer to savings (₹2000/month) and treat it as a fixed expense.
